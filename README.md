## Vendor Performance Analytics & Inventory Optimization
## Overview

This project analyzes vendor performance, inventory efficiency, and profitability using real-world retail and wholesale sales data.
The goal is to help businesses reduce losses, optimize inventory, and improve vendor decision-making through data-driven insights.

## Business Questions Answered

Which vendors and brands generate the most sales and profit?

Where is inventory capital being wasted?

Does bulk purchasing reduce unit costs?

How do high-performing and low-performing vendors differ financially?

## Key Insights

Top 10 vendors account for ~66% of total purchases, indicating high vendor dependency risk.

$2.71M in unsold inventory capital identified from slow-moving products.

Bulk purchasing lowers unit cost by ~72%, improving profitability.

Low-performing vendors have higher margins (~41%) but low sales, suggesting pricing or marketing inefficiencies.

Statistical testing confirms significant profit margin differences between vendor groups.
